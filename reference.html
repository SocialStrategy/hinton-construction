About Us

Small team.
Big attention to detail.

Hinton Construction Limited is a small, owner-operated residential construction company based in Christchurch. We specialise in architectural new builds, renovations, and alterations — and we work closely with homeowners who value craftsmanship over shortcuts.

We deliberately take on fewer projects so we can give each one the focus it deserves. When you build with Hinton, you're dealing directly with us from day one to CCC.
